I am a PhD candidate at University College London, studying the climate system response to solar geoengineering by stratospheric aerosol injection. My work aims to understand the physics of what changes to the climate we might see, particularly in the Arctic, if we were to try to cool it down by reflecting away about 1% of the incoming sunlight.
